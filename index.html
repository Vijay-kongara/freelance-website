Skill India


The Skill India Mission aims to offer market-oriented skills training to nearly 40 Crore Indians by 2022. It works to create a space for promoting the talents of youths and creating job opportunities for them. Skill India Mission funds sectors that are searching for options to recognise areas for skill development.
